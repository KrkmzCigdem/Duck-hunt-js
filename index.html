How to Play

  • - Move the aim using your mouse or arrow keys.
  • - Click or press the spacebar to shoot.
  • - Hit the moving targets to score points.
  • - Each target has different points, depending on its difficulty.
  • - Avoid getting hit by the projectiles coming from the top of the screen.
  • - You start with 3 lives; lose all lives or run out of time to end the game.
  • - Your high score will be saved, and you can try to beat it in the next round.
  • - Press 'Escape' to pause or resume the game.
